Club History

 

CSS Wanderers were founded in 1996 and have since become a force in modern football. We have fans all around the world and have won countless trophies whilst working our way up through the divisions!

Affectionally nicknamed the 'Bootstrappers', our club has become famous for our colorful approach to playing the game. Our famous gold and black kit has become synonymous with perserverence and grit.
